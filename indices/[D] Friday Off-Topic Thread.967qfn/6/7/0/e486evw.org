Nominally, both the United States and the United Kingdom explicitly adhere to option 2. (Read Justice Breyer's dissent in [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eldred_v._Ashcroft][/Eldred/ v. /Ashcroft/]] for details.)
