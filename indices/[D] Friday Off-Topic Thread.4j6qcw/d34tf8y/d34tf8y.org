Suicide Sunshine:

- 1 mango
- 10 mint leaves
- 1/2 small serrano pepper
- 2 oz Mezcal
- 1/2 oz lime juice
- 1/2 oz agave nectar

Mellow the mango and mint leaves in a wossname, a cocktail shaker. Add the lime juice, agave nectar, and Mezcal. Chop in the pepper.

Shake with ice.

Strain into glasses.

Drink.
